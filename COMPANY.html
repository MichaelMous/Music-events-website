Milestones

Music On. In its 25+ years of presence it endeavors to offer a
first-class level of service with a bespoke approach to all
audio/visual needs. The company’s highly trained personnel, using
state-of-the-art equipment, successfully organizes all sorts of
events ranging from wedding parties to live band concerts to corporate events to fashion shows etc.


1988
Events Music Productions is founded by Tolis Tilonis and Nikos Choakos.

1996
Transition from Technics MK2 to Pioneer CDJ500.

2000
DJ set for millennium party at the awarded Anassa Hotel Cyprus.

2001
Introduction of VDMX to the Greek market and Transition from cd
player to DJ so?ware. Music On is the official distributor of VDMX
to this day.

2001
Expansion of the company’s services to include events’ production beyond audiovisual.

2004
Appointment as the exclusive partner of Life Gallery Athens Hotel & Spa in entertainment and audiovisual support.

2007
Acquisition of “Dancefloor Events & Parties” company.

2008
Appointment as a supplier for Elais Unilever Hellas S.A.

2010
Appointment as a preferred partner at Radisson Athens Park Hotel.

2010
Opening event of Hellenic Motor Museum - Appointment as a preferred partner at Hellenic Motor Museum

2013
Appointment as a preferred partner at Grand Resort Lagonissi.

2013
Appointment as a preferred partner at Cape Sounio Grecotel Exclusive Resort.

2013-2014
Official TEDx Athens party organizer at Onassis Cultural Center.

2014
Appointment as a preferred partner at St. George Lycabettus Boutique Hotel.

2015
Appointment as an exclusive partner at Laas Estate.

2017
Events Music Productions rebranded as Music On.

2017
Introduction of L’Acoustics SYVA sound system to Greece.
